Major Operations of Indian Defence Forces

1. Operation Vijay (1999 – Kargil War)

Operation Vijay was launched to remove infiltrators from the Kargil sector in 1999. Indian soldiers fought bravely in extreme high-altitude conditions and successfully reclaimed occupied peaks. It remains a symbol of courage and national pride.

2. Operation Meghdoot (1984)

Operation Meghdoot secured the strategic Siachen Glacier. India became the first country to occupy the highest battlefield in the world. The operation reflects the bravery and endurance of soldiers in extreme cold conditions.

3. Operation Rahat (2013)

Operation Rahat was a major rescue mission during the Uttarakhand floods. The Indian Armed Forces rescued thousands of stranded civilians. It became one of the largest disaster relief operations in India.

4. Operation Ganga (2022)

Operation Ganga evacuated Indian citizens from Ukraine during the war. Thousands of students and civilians were safely brought back to India.

5. Surgical Strikes (2016)

The 2016 Surgical Strikes targeted terrorist launch pads across the Line of Control. The operation showed India's strong response against terrorism.
